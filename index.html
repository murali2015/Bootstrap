Mumbai Indians 2020 Champions...

Reigning champions Mumbai Indians defeated Delhi Capitals by 5 wickets in the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2020 final at the Dubai International Stadium to clinch their record-extending fifth IPL title. Chasing 157 to win, Mumbai Indians captain Rohit Sharma was the star of the show with 68 runs from 51 balls. MI also became only the second team since Chennai Super Kings to successfully defend an IPL title.
